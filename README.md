## How We Engage

We work as a focused execution partner — not an agency with layers of account managers. You deal directly with the person building and running your campaigns.
```
Discovery Call → Proposal → Onboarding → Execution → Reporting → Iteration
```

**We are a good fit if you need:**
- A website that actually converts and ranks on Google
- Google or Meta Ads managed by someone who reads the data, not just the dashboard
- A custom web platform or internal tool built and shipped fast
- A technical partner who understands both marketing and engineering

**We are not the right fit if you need:**
- A large agency with dedicated teams for every channel
- Brand identity or graphic design as a primary service
- Enterprise-scale managed services contracts
